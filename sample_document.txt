Product Name: NovaPulse Pro Wireless Earbuds

Overview:
NovaPulse Pro is a pair of premium wireless earbuds designed for professionals and audiophiles who demand both performance and comfort during long workdays. Built for all-day wear, they feature adaptive noise cancellation, crystal-clear call quality, and a 32-hour total battery life.

Key Features:
- Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) with 3 adjustable levels: Light, Deep, and Full Silence
- Transparency Mode for ambient sound awareness
- 32-hour total battery life (8 hours earbuds + 24 hours charging case)
- IPX5 water resistance rating
- Multipoint Bluetooth 5.3 connection (connect to 2 devices simultaneously)
- Touch-sensitive controls on each earbud
- Built-in voice assistant support (Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa)
- Wear detection: auto-pause when removed from ears

Audio Specifications:
- Driver size: 10mm dynamic drivers
- Frequency response: 20Hz – 20kHz
- Codec support: AAC, SBC, aptX HD
- Microphone: 6-mic array for calls (2 per earbud + 2 in case)

Target Audience:
Remote workers, business travelers, fitness enthusiasts, and music lovers aged 25–45 who want premium audio quality without the bulk of over-ear headphones.

Value Proposition:
NovaPulse Pro delivers studio-grade sound and enterprise-level call clarity in a featherlight design — the only earbuds you'll need from Monday morning meetings to Friday evening runs.

Pricing:
$149 USD. Available in Midnight Black, Arctic White, and Slate Blue.

Warranty: 2-year manufacturer warranty with free replacement for defects.
